About Me

As a seasoned designer with a unique trajectory from apparel design to UI design complemented by frontend development skills, I bring a distinctive blend of creativity and technical acumen to the field. My professional journey is a testament to my adaptability, commitment to innovation, and the continuous pursuit of excellence in the ever-evolving realm of user experience and frontend development.
